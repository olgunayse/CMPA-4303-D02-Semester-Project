Side by side property comparison

A simple example of how buyers can better understand value, trade offs, and which option fits their priorities.

Home A

More space, lower price

  • Price: $300,000
  • Location: Suburb
  • Size: 2,000 sqft
  • Best for: Buyers who want more space
Home B

Stronger location, less space

  • Price: $350,000
  • Location: City
  • Size: 1,500 sqft
  • Best for: Buyers who want a stronger location
Trade off summary

What changes between these two options?

Home A offers more square footage for a lower price, while Home B offers a more convenient location but less space. This makes the decision less about which home is “better” overall and more about what matters more to the buyer.
