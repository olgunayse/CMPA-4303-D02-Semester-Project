Project Purpose

The Approach

Real estate data can be confusing for buyers because there are so many numbers to consider at once. This tool keeps the comparison focused on the details that usually matter most.

The goal of the tool is not to choose the “perfect” property for someone. Instead, it helps users compare two homes based on affordability, space, location, and long term ownership expenses.

By simplifying the comparison, buyers can understand trade offs more clearly and make more informed decisions when looking at different homes.

Location Context

Location plays a major role in property value

Commute time, nearby amenities, schools, and neighborhood access can significantly affect long term value and overall affordability.

Why it matters

Buyers often compare homes emotionally, but this project helps bring more structure to the process. It makes the differences between two homes easier to understand.

What it supports

This tool is designed to support decision making, not replace it. It helps buyers and agents talk about value more clearly and with less confusion.
