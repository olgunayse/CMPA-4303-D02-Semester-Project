Compare Homes Smarter

Home Compare

Semester Project

Make home comparisons easier to understand.

This project is a simple web tool that helps buyers compare two homes side by side. Instead of looking at listings separately, users can view important differences in price, size, and location in one place.

Go to Comparison Page

What this project does

Compare two homes

View two properties side by side instead of switching between separate pages.

Show trade offs

Help users understand what they gain in one option and what they give up in another.

Support buyer decisions

Highlight which home may fit a buyer better depending on their priorities.

Why I made it

Buyers can easily feel overwhelmed when they are comparing homes. My goal was to create a simple layout that makes the comparison process more organized and easier to follow. This project focuses on clarity rather than showing too much information at once.
