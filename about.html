About This Project

I wanted this project to feel practical instead of just being a random demo site. Buyers usually compare homes using more than one factor at a time, such as price, space, and location.

This tool is meant to bring those factors into one view so the comparison feels easier to follow. The goal is not to say that one home is always better, but to make the differences easier to understand.

To use the project, go to the Compare page, choose a buyer priority, and press Compare.
