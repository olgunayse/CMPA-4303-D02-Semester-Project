Compare Homes Smarter
Home Compare

Semester Project

A simpler way to compare two homes

I made this project to show how comparing homes can be easier when the most important details are placed in one clear view. Instead of looking at separate listings and trying to remember everything, the user can compare price, size, and location side by side.

Open the Comparison Page

Why this tool is helpful

It keeps everything in one place

One of the hardest parts of comparing homes is going back and forth between different listings. This layout keeps the main details together so the comparison is easier to follow.

It makes trade offs easier to notice

Sometimes one home has more space but a weaker location, while another has a better location but a higher price. This project is meant to make those differences more obvious.

It helps make the decision feel less confusing

My goal was not to overload the user with too much information. I wanted it to stay simple, readable, and useful for someone trying to compare two options quickly.

What gave me the idea

I wanted to build something that felt practical and connected to real estate. A lot of people do not just compare homes by price alone, so I liked the idea of making a tool that shows how different factors can matter at the same time. I also wanted the project to feel simple and useful instead of overcomplicated.
