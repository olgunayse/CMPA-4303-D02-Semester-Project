Decision Factors

What affects long term affordability?

Listing price alone does not determine whether a property is financially realistic long term. Ownership costs can change significantly depending on taxes, interest rates, and recurring fees.

Interest Rate

Even small changes in mortgage rates can significantly increase monthly payments over time and affect the total amount paid for the property.

Property Tax

Homes with lower listing prices may still become more expensive monthly if property taxes are considerably higher in that area.

HOA Fees

Monthly HOA costs can impact affordability and should be included when comparing the true cost of ownership between properties.
