Real Estate Comparison Tool

This website is designed to help real estate agents explain property values, pricing differences, and trade offs to their clients in a clearer and more simple way.

Go to Comparison Page

About the Project

Many buyers do not fully understand why one home costs more than another. My goal is to create a website that helps break down these differences in a way that feels less overwhelming.

This project is meant to support agents in explaining homes more clearly, not replace them. It focuses on helping clients understand what they are paying for and what trade offs they may be making.

Main Goals

Explain pricing: Help users understand why homes may be priced differently.

Show trade offs: Make it easier to compare what someone gains and gives up.

Support agents: Give agents a clearer way to guide clients through decisions.
