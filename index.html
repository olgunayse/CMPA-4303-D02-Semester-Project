Compare Homes Smarter
Home Compare

Semester Project

Comparing two homes should not feel harder than the actual decision.

This project is built around one idea: make the comparison clearer. Instead of opening multiple listings and trying to remember everything, the user can look at two homes in one place and focus on the trade offs.

Open comparison page

Project idea

I wanted this to feel more practical than a random demo site. Since home buyers usually compare more than one factor at a time, I built the page around price, space, and location.

What the tool helps with

It keeps the main details visible at the same time, so the user does not have to keep switching between separate pages.

What I wanted to avoid

I did not want the page to feel overloaded or too polished. The main goal was clarity, not trying to fit in every possible feature.

Main idea behind the layout

One home may be better in price, while the other may be better in location.

That is what this tool is meant to show. It is less about picking a single “best” home and more about making the differences easier to read.
